ABOUT US..

We are working across regions in about 70 countries, with thousands of partners, and allies, supporting communities to build better lives for themselves, grow resilience and protect lives and livelihoods also in times of crisis. Our ambition is lasting impact. We make a difference by being part of a global movement for social justice. We believe in the power of people.


Managing Our Funds

Make-A-Wish International is proud of the way it manages and safeguards the generous contributions from individual donors, corporations and other organizations. Make-A-Wish International supports its day-to-day operations, in addition to its 39 affiliates, working to grant wishes across the globe. This includes helping affiliates develop resources, administer programs and referrals, navigate policies and guidelines, and brand advancement. In 2020, more than 82 cents out of every U.S. dollar given to Make-A-Wish International helped grant the wishes of 11,581 children living with critical illnesses globally. $9.1M was raised in FY20 by Make-A-Wish International and $55.8M globally by Make-A-Wish Affiliates.
